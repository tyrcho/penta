Produces 0.5 [Wood](Wood.md) per second.

Upgrade of the [Grove](Grove.md).

Home to Ents that deal 2 damage/sec to units passing on adjacent cells.

Every 10 seconds, spawns an [Elf](Elf.md).

Heals corruption (see [Corruption](../../Resources/Mort/Corruption.md)) on itself and the 8 surrounding buildings by 0.3% per second.
