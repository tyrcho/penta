Spawned by [Stonehenge](Stonehenge.md).

Moves slowly (half standard speed), like a [Zombie](../../Resources/Mort/Zombie.md).

Every 10 seconds, it stops for 3 seconds and spawns a clone of itself on the next cell of its path, then resumes walking. The new clone is 80% the size and HP of the one that made it, and can itself clone the same way — down to a minimum of 20% of the original size.

Heads out to attack the opponent like any other unit, but still counts toward its owner's Nature victory condition (Unstoppable Expansion) for as long as it's alive, even while it's attacking.
