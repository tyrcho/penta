Summoned by the [Necromancer](Necromancer.md).

Moves at normal speed.

Corrupts adjacent enemy buildings by 1% per second (see [Corruption](../../Resources/Mort/Corruption.md)).

Every time it corrupts a building, it heals the Necromancer that summoned it 1 HP per second per building corrupted (up to its maximum).
