![Base Lab](../../game/assets/labo-fondamental.png)

The only Science building buildable directly — a generic lab with no research line of its own.

Produces 0.2 [Crystal](Crystal.md) per second, with no research bonus.

Can then be upgraded into one of the five specific labs ([Nature Lab](Nature%20Lab.md), [Shadow Lab](Shadow%20Lab.md), [Research Lab](Research%20Lab.md), [Law Lab](Law%20Lab.md), [Chaos Lab](Chaos%20Lab.md)), at that lab's usual cost — see [Note on labs](../../Resources/Science/Note%20sur%20les%20laboratoires.md). This upgrade immediately grants a free level 1 of the matching research line.

An upgraded building grows 10% per research level (up to 50% larger at level 5) — the Base Lab itself always stays its base size.
