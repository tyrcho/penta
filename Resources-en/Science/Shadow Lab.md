Upgrade of the [Base Lab](Base%20Lab.md) — not directly buildable.

Produces 0.2 [Crystal](Crystal.md) per second, boosted by 75% per level.

Then keeps upgrading further in place up to level 5, the same way any other building does (same mechanism as Grove → Forest → Jungle):

| Level | Cost | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Free (granted when upgrading from the Base Lab) | +10% opponent's victory targets |
| 2 | 15 Shadow + 30 Crystal | +25% opponent's victory targets |
| 3 | 45 Shadow + 90 Crystal | +45% opponent's victory targets |
| 4 | 135 Shadow + 270 Crystal | +75% opponent's victory targets |
| 5 | 405 Shadow + 810 Crystal | +120% opponent's victory targets |
